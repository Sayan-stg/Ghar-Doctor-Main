Powerful Features for Better Healthcare

Ghar Doctor combines cutting-edge technology with accessible healthcare solutions to bring medical monitoring to every home.

Real-time Health Monitoring

Experience continuous health tracking with instant updates on vital signs. Our system monitors body temperature, heart rate, and other critical parameters 24/7, providing you with real-time insights into your health status. Never miss a critical change with our live data streaming technology.

Live Updates Every Second

ESP32 Sensor Integration

Powered by advanced ESP32 microcontrollers, our wearable sensors deliver medical-grade accuracy. The seamless IoT integration ensures reliable data collection from multiple bio-sensors, transmitting vital information wirelessly to the cloud without any manual intervention.

Medical-Grade Accuracy

Secure Data Handling

Your health data is protected with bank-level encryption. All patient information is transmitted through secure HTTPS channels and stored in encrypted databases. We comply with healthcare data privacy standards to ensure your medical records remain confidential and accessible only to authorized healthcare providers.

End-to-End Encrypted

Responsive Web Dashboard

Access your health data from any device - desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Our intuitive dashboard adapts seamlessly to any screen size, providing doctors and patients with a beautiful, easy-to-navigate interface. View trends, export reports, and share data with healthcare professionals effortlessly.

Works on All Devices

Scalable Architecture

Built to grow with your needs, our cloud-based infrastructure can support from a single patient to thousands of users simultaneously. The modular design allows easy integration of new sensors and features, making Ghar Doctor future-proof and adaptable to emerging healthcare technologies.
