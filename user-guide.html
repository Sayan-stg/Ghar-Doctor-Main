User Guide & Documentation

[cite_start]Follow these instructions to ensure accurate readings and safe usage of the Ghar Doctor sensor kit[cite: 130].

Sensor Placement

  • Ensure the device is powered on and the ESP32 light is blinking.
  • Place the temperature sensor gently against the skin (forehead or wrist).
  • Keep your finger steady on the pulse sensor for at least 15 seconds.
    • [cite_start]
  • Avoid movement during the data collection phase[cite: 132].

Common Errors

  • No Data: Check Wi-Fi connection on the ESP32 module.
  • Inaccurate Temp: Ensure the sensor is not covered by sweat or hair.
    • [cite_start]
  • Erratic Pulse: Do not press too hard on the sensor; a light touch is best[cite: 133].

Safety Precautions

  • Do not use the device on open wounds or injured skin.
  • Keep the electronic components away from water/moisture.
  • Discontinue use if the device becomes uncomfortably hot.
    • [cite_start]
  • This device is for screening only and is not a replacement for a doctor[cite: 134, 154].
